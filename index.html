Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify You. Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:

  • Account & contact information: first name and last name, nickname, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Address information: street address, city, state/province, country, and ZIP/postal code.
  • Profile information: date of birth, gender, marital status, uniform size, and profile photo.

Some of the information above (in particular gender, marital status, and date of birth) may be considered sensitive. We collect it solely to create and operate Your professional account and provide the Service, and We treat it accordingly in our app-store privacy disclosures.

Payment and Financial Information

The Service supports in-person card payments (including Tap to Pay on iPhone) through our payment processor Stripe. Card details (card number, expiry, security code) are collected and processed directly by Stripe and are not stored on Your device or on our servers. We receive and store payment metadata linked to a job or invoice — such as the payment amount, currency, status, and the Stripe transaction / payment-intent identifiers. Stripe's handling of Your payment information is governed by Stripe's privacy policy: https://stripe.com/privacy.

Location Data

With Your permission, We collect Your device's precise location while You are using the app (foreground / "when in use"). We use it to:

  • show Your current position on the job map; and
  • enable in-person card payments via Stripe Terminal / Tap to Pay, which require location access.

We do not collect location in the background. You can enable or disable location access at any time through Your device settings; disabling it may limit map and in-person payment features.

Photos, Camera and Media

With Your permission, We access Your device camera and photo library so You can attach photos to a job or report and set a profile picture. Photos You choose to attach are uploaded to and stored on our servers as part of Your job records. We do not access Your photo library for any other purpose.

Calls and Microphone (Twilio)

The Service lets You place voice calls to customers from within the app, powered by Twilio. To do this We access Your device microphone during a call and process the phone number You dial. The call is routed through Twilio; Twilio's processing is governed by Twilio's privacy policy: https://www.twilio.com/legal/privacy.

Call recording. Calls placed through the app are recorded. We record call audio for quality assurance, support, record-keeping, and dispute-resolution purposes. Recordings are processed via Twilio and retained for one (1) month, after which they are deleted. Where the law of Your or the customer's jurisdiction requires it, You are responsible for informing the other party that the call is being recorded before continuing.

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. It may include information such as the screens of the Service that You visit, the time and date of Your use, the time spent on those screens, Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, device type and model, mobile operating system, unique device identifiers, and diagnostic data.

Device Identifiers and Push Notifications

We collect a unique application instance identifier and, where You enable notifications, a push notification token issued by Firebase Cloud Messaging (Apple Push Notification service on iOS). We use these to deliver notifications related to Your jobs and account. You can disable push notifications at any time in Your device settings.

Biometric Authentication

If You enable biometric sign-in (Face ID / Touch ID / fingerprint), the biometric match is performed entirely on Your device by the operating system. We never receive, transmit, or store Your biometric data; the app only stores a credential securely in the device keychain to unlock Your session.
